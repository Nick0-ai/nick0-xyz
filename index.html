Nick0

Building the universal brain for robots.
Nicolas Philippe · 19 · Paris · Founder of 20n Research Laboratory

I started coding at sixteen in Alsace, France. No one taught me. I picked up Python because I wanted to build trading bots, and within months I was selling websites to local businesses while still in high school.

I launched GlowGum, a beauty chewing gum brand that hit 12,000 TikTok followers in fifteen days. I built Friday, an autonomous agent that could see and control any Mac like a human. I won my first hackathon, Innov Hack Paris, first place among 150 participants.

Friday proved that a brain could perceive and act on an environment in real time. The natural question: what if the environment is a physical body? That led me to found 20n Research Laboratory in January 2026, where I now build a universal brain for robots grounded in neuroscience and Active Inference.
