Building the universal brain for robots.

I founded 20n Research Laboratory, where I'm building a generative model for morphology-agnostic robot control — one architecture that adapts to any robot body at runtime, grounded in Active Inference (Friston, 2010) and Predictive Coding.

Early results: a single model controlling Walker2d, Ant, and Humanoid in MuJoCo. Zero training steps. CPU-only inference.

Before 20n, I built Friday (autonomous macOS agent), shipped PAACT to the App Store in 7 days (winning 1st at Innov Hack Paris, 150 participants), and launched GlowGum (consumer brand, 12k TikTok followers in 15 days). I've been writing code since I was 16 — websites, iOS apps, e-commerce, AI agents. Everything I know, I learned by shipping.
