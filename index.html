About

Who I am

Nick0, 19, Paris

Self-taught since 16. No formal CS education. I've been building websites, iOS apps, e-commerce brands, AI agents, and now robot control architectures — all by shipping first, learning second.

I founded 20n Research Laboratory to work on what I believe is the most important problem in robotics: one architecture that adapts to any body. The work is grounded in Active Inference and Predictive Coding.

Each project taught me something different. Freelance taught me to code. GlowGum taught me distribution. Friday taught me about perception-action loops. 20n is where it all converges.
