Current robot learning requires environment-specific training for each morphology. Transfer across bodies remains an open problem.

I'm building a generative model grounded in Active Inference (Friston, 2010) that adapts to new robot morphologies at runtime — without retraining, without reward shaping, without cloud compute.

Early results: one architecture, three MuJoCo bodies, zero retraining.
