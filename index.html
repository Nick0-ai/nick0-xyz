Self-taught since 16. No formal CS education.
Building the universal brain for robots.

I started coding at sixteen in Alsace, France. No one taught me. I picked up Python because I wanted to build trading bots, and that curiosity quickly became obsessive. Within months I was selling websites to local businesses, negotiating contracts, and managing clients while still in high school.

From there, everything accelerated. I launched GlowGum, a beauty chewing gum brand that reached 12,000 TikTok followers in fifteen days. I built NERVE, a Cloud FinOps platform, and Eve, an AI agent that creates and trains models from a single conversation. I created Friday, an autonomous agent that could see and control any Mac interface like a human.

Friday was the turning point. Controlling a screen proved that a brain could perceive and act on an environment in real time. The natural question became: what if the environment is not a screen, but a physical body? That question led me to found 20n Research Laboratory in January 2026.
