PAACT

Anti-procrastination app. Built and shipped to the App Store in 7 days. Won 1st place at Innov Hack Paris (150 participants).

January 2026 · Solo · App Store
Concept

Reduce starting friction to zero

PAACT is built around micro-commitments: break any task into 2-minute starting blocks. The app focuses on getting you to start, not on planning or organizing. Minimal UI, no feature overload.

Constraint

7 days, start to App Store

The time constraint was deliberate. 7 days forces you to ship only what matters and cut everything else. No scope creep, no premature abstraction.

  • Day 1-2: Core Data model, task creation/completion flow
  • Day 3-4: SwiftUI interface, notification scheduling
  • Day 5-6: Edge cases, polish, App Store screenshots
  • Day 7: Submission and review
