GlowGum

Skincare chewing gum brand. Built solo from concept to operations. 12,000 TikTok followers in 15 days.

2025 · Solo founder
What I did

End-to-end brand operations

Built everything solo: product concept, brand identity, supplier sourcing (MOQs, ingredient compliance), Shopify store, TikTok content strategy, paid ads, community management.

The TikTok-first distribution strategy drove all the growth. 12k followers in 15 days through organic content + targeted UGC coordination.

What it taught me

Execution under constraints

  • Distribution beats product: the best product means nothing if nobody sees it
  • Speed of iteration matters more than getting it right the first time
  • Supplier negotiation, logistics, compliance — the unglamorous work that makes a product real

GlowGum isn't a technical project, but it taught me how to ship something real, fast, with zero resources. That skill transfers directly to building anything.
