GlowGum

The first skincare chewing gum brand. Built from scratch, solo. 12,000 TikTok followers in 15 days.

2025 · Solo founder · Consumer brand
The Problem

Skincare is broken for Gen Z

The skincare industry sells complicated routines, expensive products, and promises that take months to deliver. Young people want results, but they don't want a 10-step routine.

What if you could improve your skin just by chewing gum? A product so simple it fits into the one habit everyone already has.

The Solution

Skincare you chew

GlowGum is a chewing gum infused with bioactive compounds targeting skin health from the inside. Not a supplement pill, not a cream — something you'd actually use every day without thinking about it.

  • Formulated with collagen peptides, hyaluronic acid, and vitamin E
  • Positioned for Gen Z: accessible, fun, zero-effort skincare
  • Branding designed to go viral on TikTok and Instagram
  • Competitive pricing vs. traditional skincare
Results

Viral traction from day one

What I Built

End-to-end brand operations

This wasn't just a product — it was a crash course in building a real consumer brand from nothing.

  • Brand identity: name, logo, color palette, typography, packaging design
  • Supplier negotiation: sourcing manufacturers, MOQs, ingredient compliance
  • E-commerce: Shopify store, payment processing, logistics
  • Content creation: TikTok strategy, video production, UGC coordination
  • Community management: DMs, comments, influencer outreach
  • Digital marketing: paid ads, organic growth, A/B testing
Learnings

What it taught me

GlowGum taught me more about execution than any course ever could. How to move fast, how to find product-market fit through iteration, and how to build something people actually want to talk about.

The biggest lesson: distribution beats product. Having a great product means nothing if nobody sees it. The TikTok-first strategy was the decision that made everything else work.
